My professional experiences

Consultant intern in data science, 2019

I was an intern at KPMG, in France, for 3 months. As a link between two business units (AS Bank, and Data&Analytics), I had two missions:

  • Writing an algorithm forecasting monthly invoices for edge fonds. I used statistic technics, and obtained an average accuracy of 94%!
  • Writing an algorithm affecting consultants to missions, based on different criteria (availability, profitability,...). I used a random walk.

Intern in a real estate agents team, 2018

I've been an intern for this forming team in the USA for a month. It has made me love start ups: there were a lot of things to do, they were having more and more customers, and everyone was doing whatever they could for the company. Everyone was bringing their skills, everyone was equally heard, it has been a great experience for me. My missions were:
